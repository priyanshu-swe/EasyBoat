About Us

At EasyBoat, we believe boating should be about enjoying the water, not dealing with the headaches of maintenance and logistics. That’s why we’ve created a platform to connect boat owners with trusted, skilled service providers in the Long Island Sound area.

We noticed a gap—finding reliable professionals for boat maintenance and repairs can be frustrating, expensive, and time-consuming. EasyBoat was built to solve this problem, offering a convenient, all-in-one solution for accessing the services you need when you need them.

Our vision is to foster a thriving community of boaters and service providers built on trust, convenience, and a shared love of the boating lifestyle.

Thank you for trusting EasyBoat to simplify your boating experience.
