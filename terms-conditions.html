Service Transactions and Payments

  • Users submit service requests, and providers respond with quotes.
  • The selected provider’s fee is held one day before the service.
  • Service providers must verify the boat's location with the boat owner before starting the service.
  • The provider must verify service completion via an OTP verification process.
  • The user has 24 hours to confirm the service completion before funds are released.
  • EasyBoat collects a 10 % transaction fee per completed service.
  • Stripe processes payments, deducting a 3% fee from the provider’s earnings.
  • Sales tax is applied as required by law.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

  • Users may cancel a service request within 10 minutes of pacing the order for no penalty.
  • If the user cancels after 10 minutes of placing the order, but before one week of the scheduled service, they qualify for a 80% refund.
    • 10% to the service provider.
    • 10% to EasyBoat.
  • If the user cancels within one week of the scheduled service, but prior to 48 hours before the service, they qualify for a 50 % refund.
    • 30% to the service provider.
    • 20% to EasyBoat.
  • If the user cancels within 48 hours of the scheduled service, the user does not qualify for a refund.
    • 80% to the service provider.
    • 20% to EasyBoat.
  • Disputes over completed services must be reported within 24 hours of completion.
