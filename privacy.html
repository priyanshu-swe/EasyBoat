Our Commitment to Transparency

Privacy Policy

Protecting Your Privacy

Protecting your private information is our priority. This Privacy Policy applies to www.easyboat.app and EasyBoat mobile applications (collectively, “EasyBoat”) and governs data collection and usage. By using EasyBoat, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of Your Personal Information

To provide you with our services, EasyBoat may collect the following personally identifiable information:

  • First and Last Name
  • Mailing Address
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number

If you purchase EasyBoat’s services, we collect billing and credit card information. This information is used to complete your transactions.

We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it. However, you may need to provide certain personal details when using our services, such as:

  • Registering an account
  • Participating in promotions, surveys, or contests
  • Signing up for special offers
  • Sending us inquiries or service requests
  • Submitting payment details for transactions

EasyBoat may also gather additional personal or non-personal information in the future.

Use of Your Personal Information

EasyBoat collects and uses your personal information to:

  • Operate the website and mobile application
  • Provide the services you request
  • Improve user experience and customer support
  • Notify you about new products or services from EasyBoat and affiliates

Sharing Information With Third Parties

EasyBoat does not sell, rent, or lease customer information. However, we may share data with trusted partners for:

  • Performing statistical analysis
  • Sending emails or postal mail
  • Providing customer support
  • Facilitate service provider transactions

All third-party partners are prohibited from using your personal information for any purpose other than providing services to EasyBoat and are required to maintain confidentiality.

EasyBoat may disclose your personal information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect EasyBoat’s rights and property
  • Safeguard user and public safety

Tracking User Behavior

EasyBoat may track user behavior on the website and app to determine popular services and improve user experience. This data helps us tailor content and marketing efforts.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect information about your device, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Access times
  • Referring website addresses

This information helps maintain service quality and optimize EasyBoat’s functionality.

Security of Your Personal Information

EasyBoat secures your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure using:

  • SSL Encryption: Protecting payment and personal data
  • Access Controls: Restricting sensitive data access

While we take appropriate security measures, no internet transmission is 100% secure. By using EasyBoat, you acknowledge inherent security risks.

Children Under Thirteen

EasyBoat does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are under 13, parental consent is required to use our services.

Connecting EasyBoat to Third-Party Accounts

You may link your EasyBoat account to third-party services (e.g., Google, Facebook). By doing so, you consent to the release of certain information according to your privacy settings on those platforms. You can disconnect your account at any time.

Email Communications

EasyBoat may contact you via email for service updates, promotions, and announcements. To opt out of marketing emails, click the UNSUBSCRIBE link in the email.

External Data Storage

Your data may be stored on servers provided by third-party hosting vendors.

Changes to This Policy

EasyBoat reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy. We will notify users via:

  • Email (to the address associated with your account)
  • A notice on our website

Your continued use of EasyBoat after modifications indicates acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
